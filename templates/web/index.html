About HBF Nidhi

Founded in 2017 under the Companies Act, 2013, HBF Nidhi Limited is a Public Limited Company dedicated to operating as a Nidhi company. Our core mission is to install a culture of savings and financial responsibility among our valued members.
At HBF Nidhi Limited, we provide a structured platform for savings and ensure transparent, straightforward processes for financial assistance. Our business model centres around facilitating transactions exclusively within our member community. We accept deposits and extend credit to our members, encouraging widespread savings habits. Moreover, we offer credit at more affordable rates compared to traditional banks, by ensuring financial support is accessible.
For over seven years, HBF Nidhi Limited has effectively enhanced the social and financial well-being of its members. We achieve this through fostering cooperation, promoting savings, and providing comprehensive technical and financial support.
Join us at HBF Nidhi Limited as we work together to build a secure financial future.

OUR COMPANY

HBF NIDHI LIMITED, a public limited company, was incorporated in 2017, recognizing the prevalent gap in addressing the basic functionality of banking to inculcate the habits of “Thrift Saving”. In line with the growing aspiration of a resurgent and developing economy of India and with the focus of financial inclusivity of the population by the present economic outlook, HBF Nidhi Limited aspires to address this opportunity with its innovative products and services. Our businesss model stands on the principles of transacting within members of the Nidhi company. We accept deposits from lend credit to our members and thus inculcte the habbit of savings among the masses and also provide credit at lower costs than traditional banks.

What is a nidhi company?

A nidhi company is a type of non-banking financial entity in India, primarily involved in accepting deposits and providing loans to its members. Nidhi companies primarily aim to cultivate the habit of thrift and savings among their members and facilitate mutual financial assistance.

Who can be a nidhi member?

Individuals who comply with the membership criteria set by the nidhi company, often involving regular savings contributions, can become member.

How can the interest be paid for my FD?

Interest on FDs can usually be paid at maturity .

Can I make a nomination for my FD?

Yes, you can nominate a person who will receive the proceeds of your FD in case of your demise. Nomination forms are available with HBF NIDHI Limited.

Is cheque facility available for a savings deposit account?

No, cheque facility is not available for a savings deposit account with HBF Nidhi Limited.

What are the criteria for opening a Saving account, FD , RD and Loans in HBF NIDHI Limited?

Opening a Saving account, FD , RD and Loans in HBF NIDHI Limited typically involves meeting certain criteria and following specific procedures. Here are the general criteria you would need to fulfill:

1. Membership Requirement: HBF Nidhi company is a mutual benefit societies, and often, you need to be a member of the company to avail our deposit services and other banking services .

2. Know Your Customer (KYC) Documentation: HBF Nidhi company require KYC documents to establish your identity and address. This usually includes:
Proof of identity ( Aadhaar card )

Passport-sized photographs

What is the current interest rate on FD?

You must inform HBF NIDHI Limited immediately. We will typically require an indemnity bond and other documents to issue a duplicate FD receipt.
