Fixed Deposite

HBF Fixed deposits (FDs) are an attractive mode of parking one's money safely. It offers security in terms of savings on one's income.

Recurring Deposite

HBF Nidhi Ltd. Recurring Deposit is designed to help you prepare for the future. It enables you save regularly for a certain period of time with minimum amount starting Rs. 1,000/- only and earn a higher interest rate.

Loans

HBF Nidhi Ltd Gold loans may be availed for any amount between Rs.1,000 to a maximum of Rs. 7.5 Lakhs. Loans are available for periods ranging from one month to one year.
