  • Mortgage Loan                         


At HBF Nidhi, we understand the importance of owning a home. That's why we offer mortgage loans to help you finance your dream property.

Benefits

  • Competitive Rates: We offer attractive interest rates compared to other lenders.
  • Flexible Repayment Options: Choose a repayment plan that fits your budget and financial goals.
  • Transparent Process: We explain the terms and conditions clearly, ensuring a smooth loan experience.
  • Fast Processing: Get a quick decision on your loan application.
  • Membership Benefits: Enjoy additional benefits like preferential interest rates on deposits or other financial products offered by our company (depending on company policy).

Loan Terms and Conditions:

  • Loan Amount: The maximum loan amount will depend on the value of your property, your income, and your creditworthiness. Refer to our eligibility criteria for details.
  • Interest Rates: We offer competitive fixed or floating interest rates. Please visit our branch or contact us for current rates.
  • Loan Tenure: Choose a loan tenure that aligns with your repayment capabilities.
  • Margin: We finance a certain percentage (Loan-to-Value ratio) of the property's value. The remaining amount will need to be paid as a down payment.
  • Security: The borrowed amount will be secured by a mortgage on your property.

Documents Required

  • Membership documents with HBF Nidhi
  • KYC documents (identity and address proof)
  • Income proof documents (salary slips, bank statements)
  • Property documents (ownership proof, valuation report)
