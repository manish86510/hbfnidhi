HBF Savings Account
HBF NIDHI LTD offers an attractive savings account to our members with a interest rate of 6% . We offers this savings account facility to our tie-up housing society members where banking service/solution are not easy for senior citizen, ladies & others.

Benefits of a Savings Account with HBF Nidhi LTD:

  • Competitive Interest Rates: Earn interest on your deposited funds, helping your money grow steadily.
  • Flexible Deposits: Deposit and withdraw funds conveniently as per your needs.
  • Security and Trust: Your savings are secure with us. We prioritize the safety of your funds and adhere to strict financial regulations.
  • Easy Account Management: Manage your account online or through our mobile app (if offered). Enjoy convenient features like account balance inquiries, transaction history, and fund transfers (depending on functionalities offered).
  • Membership Benefits: Being a savings account holder may entitle you to additional benefits like preferential interest rates on loans or other financial products offered by our company (depending on company policy).

Documents required for opening Savings Account:

You can open your HBF Savings Account by providing evidence of the depositor in the form of proof of identity and address as under:

    4. Elector Photo Identity Card
    5. Driving licence
    6. Ration card
  • Proof of address (any one of the following)
